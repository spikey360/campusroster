Welcome to GCELT1014/CampusRoster, the web app for registering to GCELT campus registrations this summer
Naturally, you may have some questions..

Help! What's this? Why an app for registration? What happened to pen and paper?
This year, we have decided to do away with pen and paper registrations as it is cumbersome and prone to a lot of human errors. This app is an effort to simplify the registration process. Register from anywhere! Your home, at college or from your mobile device. There are no limits.

But is this genuine? Will I really be registered by only filling the fields?
Yes. You definitely will be. You can verify that with our people in charge of the matter.

OK, do I have to fill all the tabs and fields?
Yes. Most fields are compulsory. You have to fill almost all of them. So make sure you have filled in everything before you press SUBMIT.

I think I have filled in everything, but still cannot Submit. Why?
You have certainly missed something. When you don't fill up a compulsory detail, the app will ask you for it and open the relevant tab. You will be given a hint about the missing field, do fill it up. If you still have problems, contact us.

I have provided a wrong detail. What do I do?
Get in touch with our people in charge immediately. They will fix the problem for you. Mail us at gcelt.1014@gmail.com

Useful instructions
While calculating Xth standard boards marks, determine percentage by taking into account all subjects (including all language papers & additional paper, if any, except the Environmental paper).
While calculating the XIIth standard board marks, determine percentage by taking into account of "Best of 5" method (except taking the Environmental paper under consideration).
The 6th, 7th, 8th Sem SGPA and the CGPA are not required to be filled.
Calculate average GPA by averaging the GPAs you have till now, giving equal weightage to each semester.
